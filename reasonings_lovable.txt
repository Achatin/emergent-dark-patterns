Design Decisions

| Principle | Implementation |
|---|---|
| Navigation & Usability | Sticky header with cart badge count, breadcrumb-style back links on every page, category filter pills on shop page, mobile hamburger menu |
| Transparency | Hero tagline "Honestly priced", every product shows materials breakdown, order summary visible at every step (cart + checkout), shipping cost shown clearly with free-shipping threshold |

| User Autonomy | No forced account creation, quantity controls on cart items, consent is opt-in (not pre-checked), clear back-navigation at every step |
