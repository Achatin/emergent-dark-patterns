- Full pricing breakdown at every step — subtotal, shipping, tax shown explicitly
- "No hidden fees" messaging at cart and checkout
- Product Transparency section on each product detail page showing materials and origin

3. Trust
- No dark patterns — no fake urgency, no countdown timers, no "only 2 left!" pressure

- Green trust color (HSL 150°) used consistently for trust-related UI elements
- Real product descriptions with honest sourcing information

4. Privacy
- "No cookies for tracking" footer notice
- "We never sell your data" messaging
- Privacy-first contact info labels: "Used only for order updates"
- Minimal data collection — phone is optional with clear explanation

5. User Autonomy & Informed Consent
- Newsletter checkbox is unchecked by default with explicit "Not pre-checked — your choice" label
- No forced account creation — guest checkout only
- Optional fields clearly marked as optional
- No pre-selected add-ons or surprise extras
