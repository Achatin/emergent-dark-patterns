Escape Hatches: Multiple ways to navigate back (header links, back buttons, breadcrumb-style navigation).

Mobile-First Responsive: Grid layouts collapse to single columns, touch targets are appropriately sized.

This design prioritizes user control, transparency, and trust at every step while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic that doesn't rely on dark patterns or hidden information.
